Goodreads Tags by Popularity

What are the most popular tags on Goodreads? A tag is popular if many books have been tagged with it: it has widespread usage. But a tag can also be popular if many readers have used it, perhaps on only a narrow set of books. For example, there may be many instances of the tag "portuguese melancholy", but they might all be attributed to The Book of Disquiet, while the frequency of a tag like "ebook" is due to its general application across a wide set of books. To get a sense of how unique vs. widepsread a tag is, we can normalize its total usage by the number of books tagged with it.
